About Cryptid Control

You are called in to capture Cryptid by the company you work for: Cryptid Control.

While in the HubWorld you can enter the portals to go to the levels to fight enemies and capture their bosses, the Cryptids.

We have three bosses, Mothman, Siren, and the Fresno Nightcrawler.

Capture all three and you win.

This is our final project for my game production class. We used Unity Engine and at this point into the semester, I was more confident in my skill using GitHub as well as programming the menus and cutscenes. I learnt that I wanted to stick to the technical side of design.
