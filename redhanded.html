About Red Handed

You've been caught red handed!

Red Handed is a stealth game where an art thief must steal a priceless statue from a heavily guarded art museum.

This was a project for game production where we were required to have a hallway and two guards. Our game was developed on Unity engine and I worked on programming UI, interactions with objects, learned how to use keyframes to animate opening and closing the doors, as well as fixing many errors in GitHub.
